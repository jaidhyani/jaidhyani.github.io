Bottom line

Phase 2 of the roadmap — identity, search, retention, health, metrics, auth separation — is largely already in the PR queue. The outstanding work is Phase 3–4: scaling, multi-provider, resume, and lossless capture. The multi-provider question is strategic, not implementation-time-bounded, and the doc correctly identifies it as the make-or-break item.
